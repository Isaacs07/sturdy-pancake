# Partner Questions

This page holds partner-led questions that may help test, refine, or challenge my research lenses.

The aim is not to force every partner question into the PhD. The aim is to use them carefully to understand where my lenses become practical, useful, limited, or unclear.

## Purpose of this page

- Track emerging partner questions
- Connect partner needs to my research lenses
- Identify which questions are relevant to the PhD pathway
- Separate useful examples from distractions
- Prepare focused discussion points for supervision

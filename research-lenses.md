# Research Lenses

This section holds the main lenses currently shaping my PhD pathway.

The lenses are not fixed theories at this stage. They are working frames that help me organise what I am noticing, reading, designing, and testing.

## Current lenses

| Lens | Working focus |
|---|---|
| Access-first visualisation | How meaning can be made legible for more people from the start |
| Decision under pressure | How visualisation supports interpretation, comparison, prioritisation, and action under constraint |
| Embodied, multisensory, and communicative interaction | How data and meaning can be experienced beyond conventional screen-based visualisation |

## Questions across the lenses

- Where do these lenses overlap?
- Which partner questions make each lens practical?
- Which lens is strongest at this stage?
- Which lens needs more reading or testing?
- What might need to be combined, renamed, or removed?

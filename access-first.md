# Access-First Lens

## Working definition

Access-first visualization begins with the question of who can read, use, and make meaning from a visualization.

It treats accessibility as part of the design brief, not an adjustment added at the end.

## What this lens helps me look at

- Legibility
- Colour independence
- Redundant cues
- Structure and hierarchy
- Alternative routes to meaning
- Users working under constraint
